sample file<
track start
line
line
line
.
..

track end
>

line=time in seconds with 1 digit(ex.0.0,1.0,4.9,70.1):item:item:item:item
constraint 1-4 items
item=track id(0-3):0:scores(float)

notice that line should be in the increasing order of time(each line must have unique time) and
item should be in the increasing order of trackid(each item must have unique trackid)!!!
